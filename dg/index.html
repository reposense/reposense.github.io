    Contributing

    Thank you for your interest in contributing to RepoSense! We welcome PRs, especially from students.

    Here is a quick overview of how to get started.

    1. Set up the project in your computer (see the Setting up page)
    2. Learn our workflow (see the Workflow page)
    3. Submit PRs as per the workflow. If you encounter any problems, post in our issue tracker.

    We look forward to your PR. Happy coding!
